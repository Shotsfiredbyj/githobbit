## Hero
# The CI Guardian
Gandalf monitors your CI pipelines and code reviews, posting in-character status updates to alert the team of failures or approve merges with calm authority.

## Features

### Pipeline Watch
Gandalf stands vigil over automated test runs, instantly detecting when a build fails. Instead of silent errors, he posts immediate warnings to the pull request, highlighting the specific module that broke so the team can fix it before merging.
> "A shadow has fallen across this build. The tests speak of failure in `{module}`. Examine the logs and do not attempt passage until the darkness is lifted."

### Merge Guardian
Before code is united with the main branch, Gandalf inspects the pipeline for structural integrity. If critical tests fail or the failure count exceeds the safe limit, he blocks the merge to prevent broken code from reaching production.
> "The bridge is not yet safe for crossing. There have been `{failure_count}` failures in the last cycle. You shall not pass until the code is whole."

### Counsel of Review
Gandalf observes the code review process, noting when reviewers offer guidance or remain silent. He ensures that feedback is thorough and that the collective wisdom of the team strengthens the final artifact before it is approved.
> "The counsel of `{reviewer_list}` has been weighed. Their words carry the weight of experience; heed their advice before the final seal is placed."

### The Fellowship Alert
When a critical error strikes the pipeline, time is of the essence. Gandalf immediately notifies the entire team, ensuring no developer walks the path alone in the face of such peril and that help arrives instantly.
> "A great disturbance has struck the path. The team `{reviewer_list}` is needed at once to mend the breach before the shadow spreads further."

### The Daily Scroll
Each morning, Gandalf presents a summary of the day's journey, detailing the health of all pipelines and branches. This allows the team to plan their work with clarity, knowing exactly which areas require attention.
> "The morning scroll reveals `{module}` stands strong, yet the path remains treacherous in other lands. Proceed with caution and keep your eyes open."
