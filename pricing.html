Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Shire tier really free?

Yes, the Shire is free forever with no hidden fees, offering full access to core review tools for solo travellers.

Can I try a paid tier before committing?

Absolutely. You can explore the features of Rivendell or Gondor for 14 days with a full refund if the path isn't right.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

You keep all features until the end of your current billing cycle, after which your account gracefully reverts to the Shire tier.

Do I need technical expertise to configure Rivendell?

No wizardry required; our intuitive dashboard lets any team member set up custom rules in minutes.

How is billing calculated for multiple repositories?

We charge a small toll only for the private repositories you actively manage, keeping costs predictable as you scale.

What if my organisation needs custom compliance rules?

The Valinor team builds a bespoke plan tailored to your specific security and legal requirements.
